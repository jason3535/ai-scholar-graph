法国工程师，Mistral AI 联合创始人兼 CTO，主导公司技术方向。他在巴黎综合理工学院结识联合创始人 Arthur Mensch 和 Guillaume Lample，并在 Facebook AI Research 巴黎实验室工作约八年（2015-2023），先后任博士生和研究工程师，参与了 Meta LLaMA 大模型家族的研发。2023 年 4 月与 Mensch、Lample 共同创办 Mistral AI，构建开放高效的基础模型。
French engineer and co-founder and CTO of Mistral AI,leading the company's technical direction. He met co-founders Arthur Mensch and Guillaume Lample at Ecole Polytechnique,and spent roughly eight years (2015-2023) at Facebook AI Research Paris,first as a PhD student and later as a research engineer,where he contributed to Meta's LLaMA family and published research on large-scale model training. In April 2023 he co-founded Mistral AI alongside Mensch and Lample to build open and efficient foundation models.
