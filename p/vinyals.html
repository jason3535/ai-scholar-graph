Oriol Vinyals

Google DeepMind

Google DeepMind 研究副总裁 · Seq2Seq 联合创造者

nlprl
生于智利，在西班牙加泰罗尼亚长大。UC Berkeley 博士。加入 Google Brain 和 DeepMind。与 Sutskever 和 Quoc Le 联合发明了 Seq2Seq 模型（2014）——通往现代 LLM 的基础架构之一。领导了 AlphaStar（2019，击败星际争霸 II 顶级玩家）。现任 Google DeepMind 研究副总裁，联合领导 Gemini 开发。
Born in Chile, raised in Catalonia, Spain. Earned a BS from UC Berkeley, MS from UC San Diego, and PhD from UC Berkeley (2016) under Nelson Morgan. Joined Google Brain and later DeepMind. Co-invented the sequence-to-sequence model (2014, with Sutskever and Quoc Le) -- one of the foundational architectures leading to modern LLMs. Led AlphaStar (2019), the AI that defeated top StarCraft II players. Now VP of Research at Google DeepMind, co-leading Gemini development. One of the most prolific researchers in modern AI with contributions spanning NLP, computer vision, and game AI.
