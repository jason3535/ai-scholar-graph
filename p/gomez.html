Aidan Gomez

Cohere

Transformer 联合发明人 · Cohere CEO

nlp
以本科实习生身份在 Google Brain 工作期间，成为《Attention Is All You Need》（2017）最年轻的合著者。这段参与 Transformer 研究的经历启发他于2019年创办 Cohere——一家专注企业级大语言模型的公司，总部位于多伦多。在他领导下，Cohere 成为企业 AI 的重要玩家，提供多语言模型，强调数据隐私和本地化部署。
As an undergraduate intern at Google Brain, Gomez became the youngest co-author of 'Attention Is All You Need' (2017). The experience of working on the Transformer inspired him to found Cohere in 2019, an enterprise-focused LLM company headquartered in Toronto. Under his leadership as CEO, Cohere has become a major player in enterprise AI, offering multilingual models with a focus on data privacy and on-premises deployment.
