deep-learning
美国创业者，2016 年 19 岁从麻省理工辍学创办数据标注公司 Scale AI，将其打造成各大 AI 实验室人工标注训练数据的关键供应商，并成为最年轻的白手起家亿万富翁之一。2025 年 Meta 斥资约 140 亿美元入股 Scale AI，并请他执掌新成立的超级智能实验室、出任首席 AI 官，多位资深研究者（如 Andrew Tulloch）向其汇报。1997 年生于新墨西哥州，父母为物理学家。
American entrepreneur who founded the data-labeling company Scale AI in 2016 at age 19 after dropping out of MIT, building it into a key supplier of human-labeled training data for leading AI labs and becoming one of the youngest self-made billionaires. In 2025 Meta invested roughly $14 billion for a large stake in Scale AI and brought Wang in to lead its new Superintelligence Labs as Chief AI Officer, with senior researchers such as Andrew Tulloch reporting to him. Born in 1997 in New Mexico to physicist parents.
