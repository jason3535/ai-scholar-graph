Percy Liang

Stanford

HELM 基准创造者 · Together AI 创始人

nlpsafety
斯坦福教授，在大语言模型评估和问责方面成为最重要的声音之一。创建了 HELM（大语言模型整体评估，2022），这是评估大语言模型最全面、最标准化的基准，涵盖准确性、鲁棒性、公平性和毒性等多个维度。领导了斯坦福 Alpaca 项目（2023），证明了用 GPT 生成的数据微调的小模型可以匹敌更大的模型。联合创办 Together AI，致力于开源 AI 基础设施的民主化。
Stanford professor who became one of the most important voices in LLM evaluation and accountability. Created HELM (Holistic Evaluation of Language Models, 2022), the most comprehensive and standardized benchmark for evaluating large language models across multiple dimensions including accuracy, robustness, fairness, and toxicity. Led the Stanford Alpaca project (2023), demonstrating that a small model fine-tuned on GPT-generated data could match larger models. Co-founded Together AI to democratize access to open-source AI infrastructure.
