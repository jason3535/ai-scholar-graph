Jakob Uszkoreit

Inceptive

Transformer 联合发明人 · 生物AI创业者

nlp
父亲 Hans Uszkoreit 是著名计算语言学家。在 Google 合著了 Transformer 论文（2017）。在看到 Transformer 能够学习语言的语法后，他产生了一个洞见：Transformer 同样可以学习生物学的语法。创办 Inceptive，将 Transformer 架构应用于 RNA 分子设计和药物发现，这是他参与创造的架构最具创意的跨领域应用之一。
Son of renowned computational linguist Hans Uszkoreit. Co-author of the Transformer paper (2017) at Google. After seeing how Transformers could learn the grammar of language, had the insight that they could also learn the grammar of biology. Founded Inceptive to apply Transformer architectures to RNA molecule design for drug discovery, representing one of the most creative cross-domain applications of the architecture he helped create.
