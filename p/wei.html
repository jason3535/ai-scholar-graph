Google Brain 研究者，做出了关于大语言模型的两个最具影响力的发现。第一，思维链提示（2022）：发现仅仅在提示中加入「让我们一步步思考」就能大幅提升 LLM 的推理能力，这一技术现在几乎被所有 AI 应用所采用。第二，涌现能力（2022）：证明某些能力会随着模型规模的增大而突然、不可预测地出现，从根本上改变了该领域对 Scaling 的思考方式。后加入 OpenAI，从事下一代模型的推理能力研究。
Researcher at Google Brain who made two of the most influential discoveries about large language models. First, chain-of-thought prompting (2022): showing that simply adding 'let's think step by step' to prompts dramatically improves LLM reasoning, a technique now used in virtually every AI application. Second, emergent abilities (2022): demonstrating that certain capabilities appear suddenly and unpredictably as models scale, fundamentally changing how the field thinks about scaling. Joined OpenAI to work on reasoning capabilities for next-generation models.
