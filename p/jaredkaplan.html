Anthropic

理论物理学家转 AI 研究。约翰霍普金斯大学教授。联合发表了里程碑论文《神经语言模型的缩放定律》（2020），揭示了模型规模、数据、算力与性能之间的幂律关系——这一洞察指导了 GPT-3/4 的开发。2021年与 Dario Amodei 等前 OpenAI 研究者共同创办 Anthropic。领导缩放和对齐核心研究。
Theoretical physicist turned AI researcher. Johns Hopkins University professor. Co-authored the landmark 'Scaling Laws for Neural Language Models' (2020) paper that showed power-law relationships between model size, data, compute and performance — foundational insight that guided GPT-3/4 development. Co-founded Anthropic in 2021 with Dario Amodei and other ex-OpenAI researchers. Leads core research on scaling and alignment.
