AI 研究者，2016 年在南加州大学获学士，随后加入 Google Brain，提出了用强化学习自动搜索神经网络结构的神经架构搜索（NAS），是 AutoML 的关键人物之一。后加入 OpenAI 任研究副总裁（后训练方向），其团队从事对齐、工具调用、评测研究，并训练了部署到 ChatGPT 的模型。2025 年 2 月与 Mira Murati 共同创办 Thinking Machines Lab 并任 CTO，后于 2025 年底回归 OpenAI。
AI researcher who earned his BS from USC in 2016 and joined Google Brain,where he developed Neural Architecture Search (NAS) using reinforcement learning to automate neural network design. He later joined OpenAI as VP of Research (Post-Training),where his team worked on alignment,tool use,evaluations and trained the models shipped into ChatGPT. In February 2025 he co-founded Thinking Machines Lab with Mira Murati and served as its CTO,before returning to OpenAI in late 2025.
