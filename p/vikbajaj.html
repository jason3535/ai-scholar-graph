Canadian-American scientist and co-CEO (with Jeff Bezos) of Prometheus, the industrial-AI startup that raised $12B at a $41B valuation in June 2026 to build an 'artificial general engineer' for the physical world. With a physical-chemistry PhD from MIT, he co-founded Google Life Sciences (later Verily, where he was Chief Science Officer), then served as CSO of Grail and CEO of Foresite Labs, leaving in early 2026 to focus on Prometheus. Also an adjunct professor at Stanford.
