1986年生于俄罗斯下诺夫哥罗德。5岁随家人移民以色列（在耶路撒冷长大），就读以色列开放大学。16岁移居加拿大，仅上了一个月高中就凭实力直接以大三身份进入多伦多大学。获数学学士（2005）、计算机科学硕士（2007）和博士（2013），导师均为 Geoffrey Hinton。合著了 AlexNet（2012）和 Seq2Seq 论文（2014）。2012年在斯坦福跟随 Andrew Ng 短期做博后。2013年通过 DNNresearch 被收购加入 Google Brain。2015年联合创办 OpenAI 并担任首席科学家，是 GPT 系列的核心架构师。2023年11月参与了 Sam Altman 的董事会罢免事件，后表示后悔。2024年5月离开 OpenAI，同年6月创办安全超级智能公司 SSI。2024年9月以 50 亿美元估值融资 10 亿美元，2025年4月再以 320 亿美元估值融资 20 亿美元（Greenoaks 领投约 5 亿，a16z、Lightspeed、DST、Alphabet、Nvidia 跟投），全程没有任何已发布产品、团队仅约 20 人，押注的是他的个人履历。
Born 1986 in Nizhny Novgorod, Russia. Immigrated to Israel at age 5 (grew up in Jerusalem), attended the Open University of Israel. Moved to Canada at 16 and was so advanced that after only one month of Canadian high school, he was admitted to the University of Toronto as a third-year undergraduate. Earned BS in Mathematics (2005), MS (2007), and PhD (2013), all at U of T under Geoffrey Hinton. Co-authored AlexNet (2012) and the sequence-to-sequence paper (2014). Did a brief postdoc with Andrew Ng at Stanford (2012), then joined Google Brain via the DNNResearch acquisition (2013). Co-founded OpenAI in 2015, serving as Chief Scientist and key architect of the GPT series. Participated in the Sam Altman board ouster (Nov 2023) but later expressed regret. Departed OpenAI in May 2024 and co-founded Safe Superintelligence Inc. (SSI) in June 2024. SSI raised $1B at a $5B valuation (Sept 2024), then $2B more at a $32B valuation (April 2025, led by Greenoaks with ~$500M, alongside a16z, Lightspeed, DST, Alphabet and Nvidia), all with no released product and roughly 20 staff, a pure bet on Sutskever's track record.
