华裔视觉研究者。UCSD 博士。创造 ConvNeXt 和 DiT（2023），DiT 成为 OpenAI Sora 的基础架构。2026年加入 LeCun 的 AMI Labs 任首席科学官。
Chinese-born computer vision researcher. Earned his PhD from UC San Diego. Best known for creating ConvNeXt (modernizing CNNs to match Transformer performance) and Diffusion Transformers (DiT, 2023) -- the architecture that replaced U-Nets in diffusion models and became the foundation for OpenAI's Sora video generation. Previously a researcher at Facebook AI Research (FAIR) and assistant professor at NYU. In 2026, joined Yann LeCun's AMI Labs as Chief Science Officer, working on world models.
