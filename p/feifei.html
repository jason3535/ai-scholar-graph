李飞飞 Fei-Fei LiFei-Fei Li

Stanford

ImageNet 创造者 · 斯坦福 HAI 联合主任Creator of ImageNet · Stanford HAI Co-Director

vision
1976年生于北京，在四川成都长大。16岁时随父母移民美国新泽西州 Parsippany。就读 Parsippany 高中期间，周末在家人的干洗店帮忙工作。获普林斯顿大学物理学学士（1999，全额奖学金），加州理工学院电气工程硕士和博士（2005，导师 Pietro Perona 和 Christof Koch）。2007年在普林斯顿任助理教授时启动了 ImageNet 项目，利用 Amazon Mechanical Turk 标注了 1400 万+张图片、覆盖 22000 个类别。她创办的 ImageNet 大规模视觉识别挑战赛在 AlexNet 2012年获胜后成为深度学习革命的催化剂。加入斯坦福任教（2009），获终身教职（2012），正教授（2018）。曾任斯坦福 AI 实验室主任（2013-2018），兼任 Google Cloud AI/ML 首席科学家（2017-2018）。联合创办斯坦福 HAI。2024年创办 World Labs。2025年获伊丽莎白女王工程奖。2025 年 11 月 World Labs 发布首款商业产品 Marble——首个能由文本、图像、视频或 3D 布局生成并保持一致、可自由探索的 3D 环境的世界模型；2026 年 2 月再融资 10 亿美元扩展「空间智能」，瞄准游戏、视觉特效、VR 与机器人等场景。
Born 1976 in Beijing, grew up in Chengdu, Sichuan, China. Immigrated to Parsippany, New Jersey at age 16 with her parents. Worked weekends at her family's dry-cleaning business while attending Parsippany High School. Earned a BA in Physics from Princeton University (1999, on a full scholarship), then an MS and PhD in Electrical Engineering from Caltech (2005) under Pietro Perona and Christof Koch. As an assistant professor at Princeton (2007), launched the ImageNet project, using Amazon Mechanical Turk to label 14M+ images across 22K categories. The ImageNet Large Scale Visual Recognition Challenge she created became the catalyst for the deep learning revolution when AlexNet won it in 2012. Joined Stanford as faculty (2009), tenured (2012), full professor (2018). Directed Stanford AI Lab (2013-2018), took a sabbatical as Chief Scientist of AI/ML at Google Cloud (2017-2018). Co-founded Stanford HAI (Human-Centered AI). Founded World Labs (2024) with Justin Johnson, Christoph Lassner and Ben Mildenhall. Won the Queen Elizabeth Prize for Engineering (2025). In November 2025 World Labs launched Marble — its first commercial product and the first world model that generates and maintains consistent, explorable 3D environments from text, image, video or 3D layouts — and raised $1B in February 2026 to scale 'spatial intelligence,' targeting gaming, VFX, VR and robotics.
