Sepp Hochreiter

JKU Linz / ELLIS

LSTM 联合发明人

deep-learning
奥地利计算机科学家。慕尼黑工业大学，1991年在 Schmidhuber 指导下完成毕业论文——首次发现了循环神经网络中的梯度消失问题。这直接催生了 LSTM（1997）。林茨约翰·开普勒大学教授，创办了 ELLIS Linz 单元和 JKU 机器学习研究所。
Austrian computer scientist. Studied computer science at the Technical University of Munich, completing his diploma thesis under Juergen Schmidhuber in 1991 -- in which he first identified the vanishing gradient problem in recurrent neural networks. This led to the invention of LSTM (1997, with Schmidhuber), which solved the problem and became the dominant architecture for sequence modeling for two decades. Professor at Johannes Kepler University Linz (Austria). Founded the ELLIS Unit Linz and the Institute for Machine Learning at JKU. Also contributed to bioinformatics with DeepTox.
