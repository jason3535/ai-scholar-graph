Arthur Mensch

Mistral AI

Mistral AI 联合创始人兼 CEO

nlp
1992年生于巴黎。父亲数学家，母亲计算机科学家。Ecole Polytechnique 应用数学和计算机科学硕士，ENS Paris-Saclay 数学视觉学习硕士。机器学习和功能性磁共振成像博士，之后做了2年数学博后。2020年底加入 DeepMind 巴黎，从事大语言模型、多模态和检索增强架构。2023年5月与 Guillaume Lample 和 Timothee Lacroix（均为前 Meta FAIR）联合创办 Mistral AI。一年内达到数十亿美元估值，成为欧洲 AI 冠军。以开源权重模型（Mixtral、Mistral Large）闻名。
Born 1992 in Paris. Father a mathematician, mother a computer scientist. Studied at Lycee Hoche (prep school), then Ecole Polytechnique (MS Applied Mathematics and Computer Science, 2011-2015), and Ecole Normale Superieure Paris-Saclay (MS Mathematics, Vision and Learning, 2015). PhD in machine learning and functional MRI, followed by a 2-year postdoc in mathematics. Joined DeepMind Paris in late 2020, working on large language models, multimodal systems, and retrieval-augmented architectures. Left in May 2023 to co-found Mistral AI with Guillaume Lample and Timothee Lacroix (both ex-Meta FAIR). Mistral became Europe's AI champion, reaching a multi-billion dollar valuation within a year. Known for open-weight models (Mixtral, Mistral Large) and strategic partnerships with Microsoft, Salesforce, and Nvidia.
