黄仁勋 Jensen Huang

NVIDIA

NVIDIA 联合创始人兼 CEO

deep-learning
台裔美籍工程师，1993 年联合创办 NVIDIA 并担任 CEO 至今。1963 年生于台南，幼年移民美国，先后毕业于俄勒冈州立大学与斯坦福。NVIDIA 的 GPU 与 CUDA 平台成为深度学习革命的算力引擎——2012 年 AlexNet 即在 NVIDIA GPU 上训练，如今几乎所有大模型都依赖其芯片训练。在他带领下，NVIDIA 借生成式 AI 算力浪潮成长为全球市值最高的公司之一，市值突破数万亿美元。
Taiwanese-American engineer who co-founded NVIDIA in 1993 and has led it as CEO ever since. Born in Tainan in 1963, he emigrated to the US as a child and earned degrees from Oregon State and Stanford. NVIDIA's GPUs and the CUDA platform became the computational engine of the deep learning revolution — AlexNet (2012) was trained on NVIDIA GPUs, and today essentially all large AI models are trained on them. Under his leadership NVIDIA grew into one of the world's most valuable companies, surpassing a multi-trillion-dollar market capitalization on the back of the generative-AI compute boom.
