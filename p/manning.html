Christopher Manning

Stanford

斯坦福 NLP 组主任

nlp
澳大利亚裔计算语言学家。在澳大利亚国立大学获学位，后在斯坦福获语言学博士（1994）。加入斯坦福任教，成为 NLP 领域最具影响力的人物之一。合著了经典教科书《统计自然语言处理基础》（1999）和《信息检索导论》（2008）。领导斯坦福 NLP 组，产出了业界广泛使用的工具（Stanford CoreNLP、Stanford Parser）。主导开发了 GloVe 词向量（2014）。与 Fei-Fei Li 共同担任斯坦福 HAI 联合主任。他的教学和研究培养了多代 NLP 研究者。
Australian-born computational linguist. Earned degrees from the Australian National University, then a PhD in Linguistics from Stanford (1994). Joined Stanford's faculty and became one of the most influential figures in NLP. Co-authored the foundational textbook 'Foundations of Statistical Natural Language Processing' (1999) and 'Introduction to Information Retrieval' (2008). Directed the Stanford NLP Group, producing tools (Stanford CoreNLP, Stanford Parser) used across the industry. Led the development of GloVe word embeddings (2014). Serves as Co-Director of Stanford HAI alongside Fei-Fei Li. His teaching and research have shaped multiple generations of NLP researchers.
