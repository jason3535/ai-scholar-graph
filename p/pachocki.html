Polish-born. MIT PhD in theoretical CS. Joined OpenAI 2017 as research scientist. Led alignment and reasoning research. Became Head of Alignment, then promoted to Chief Scientist in mid-2024 after Ilya Sutskever's departure. Key architect behind o1 and o3 reasoning models. Works alongside Mark Chen as co-leader of OpenAI research.
