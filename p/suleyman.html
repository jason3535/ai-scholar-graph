1984年生于伦敦，父亲是叙利亚裔出租车司机，母亲是英国护士。19岁从牛津辍学。在进入 AI 之前，联合创办了一个电话咨询服务，还在伦敦市长办公室担任政策官员。2010年与 Hassabis 和 Legg 联合创办 DeepMind，领导应用 AI 部门。DeepMind 被 Google 收购后，担任 Google 副总裁。2022年创办 Inflection AI，推出 Pi 聊天机器人。2024年微软以6.5亿美元的交易雇佣 Suleyman 和 Inflection 大部分团队，使他成为微软 AI CEO。2023年出版《即将到来的浪潮》一书，讨论 AI 风险。
Born 1984 in London to a Syrian-born cab driver father and English nurse mother. Dropped out of Oxford at 19. Before AI, co-founded a telephone counseling service and worked as a policy officer at the Mayor of London's office. Co-founded DeepMind (2010) with Hassabis and Legg, leading the applied AI division. After DeepMind was acquired by Google, moved to a VP role at Google. Founded Inflection AI in 2022, creating the Pi chatbot. In 2024, Microsoft hired Suleyman and most of Inflection's team in a $650M deal, making him CEO of Microsoft AI. Published 'The Coming Wave' (2023), a book on AI risks.
