Christian Szegedy

Inception/GoogLeNet 创造者 · xAI 联合创始人

deep-learningvision
匈牙利裔。波恩大学博士。在 Google 创造 Inception 架构（GoogLeNet）并与 Goodfellow 开创对抗样本研究。开发 Batch Normalization。2023年联合创办 xAI，2025年2月离开。
Hungarian-born computer scientist. PhD from the University of Bonn. Joined Google Research where he created the Inception architecture (GoogLeNet), winner of ILSVRC 2014, and pioneered the study of adversarial examples in neural networks alongside Ian Goodfellow. Also developed Batch Normalization, a technique now used in virtually all deep networks. Left Google to co-found xAI in 2023. Departed xAI in February 2025.
