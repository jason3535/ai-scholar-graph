美国创业者与工程师，麻省理工辍学后联合创办 Anysphere，即 AI 代码编辑器 Cursor 的母公司。Cursor 于 2023 年发布，将前沿大模型与深度编辑器集成——多文件编辑、Agent 式补全、理解整个代码库的对话——结合，成为史上增长最快的开发者工具之一，年化收入突破数亿美元。公司 2025 年以约 100 亿美元估值融资，约 1996 年出生的特鲁尔由此成为头部 AI 编程公司中最年轻的掌舵者之一。
American entrepreneur and engineer who co-founded Anysphere, the company behind the AI code editor Cursor, after studying at MIT. Launched in 2023, Cursor pairs frontier LLMs with deep editor integration — multi-file edits, agentic completion, and codebase-aware chat — and became one of the fastest-growing developer tools ever, crossing hundreds of millions in ARR. The company raised at a roughly $10B valuation in 2025, making Truell, born around 1996, one of the youngest leaders of a major AI-coding company.
