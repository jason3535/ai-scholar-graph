Alex Krizhevsky

Independent

AlexNet 创造者

deep-learningvision
生于乌克兰，在加拿大长大。多伦多大学 Geoffrey Hinton 门下的博士生。2012年与 Hinton 和 Ilya Sutskever 共同构建了 AlexNet——一个深度卷积神经网络，以压倒性优势赢得 ImageNet 竞赛（错误率 15.3%，第二名 26.2%）。这一结果被普遍认为是引爆现代深度学习时代的时刻，证明了 GPU 训练的深度网络可以大幅超越传统计算机视觉方法。三人联合创办 DNNresearch，2013年被 Google 收购。此后保持相对低调。
Born in Ukraine, grew up in Canada. PhD student under Geoffrey Hinton at the University of Toronto. In 2012, built AlexNet with Hinton and Ilya Sutskever, a deep convolutional neural network that won the ImageNet competition by a massive margin (error rate of 15.3% vs. the runner-up's 26.2%). This result is widely considered the moment that ignited the modern deep learning era, proving that GPU-trained deep networks could dramatically outperform traditional computer vision. The three co-founded DNNresearch, which was acquired by Google in 2013. Has maintained a relatively low public profile since.
