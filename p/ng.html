deep-learning
1976年生于伦敦，父母为香港移民（父亲是血液学家，母亲是艺术行政人员）。1984年随家人移居新加坡，就读精英学校莱佛士书院。在卡内基梅隆大学获计算机科学、统计学和经济学三重学士（1997），MIT 获 EECS 硕士（1998），UC Berkeley 获计算机科学博士（2002，导师 Michael I. Jordan）。2002年加入斯坦福任教，其 CS229 机器学习课程成为校内最受欢迎的课程（1000+学生）。2011年与 Jeff Dean 联合创办 Google Brain，该实验曾用 16000 个 CPU 训练出能从 YouTube 视频中识别猫的网络。2012年与 Daphne Koller 联合创办 Coursera，为 800 万+学生普及教育。担任百度首席科学家（2014-2017），领导 1300 名 AI 研究员。创办 DeepLearning.AI（2017）、Landing AI（2018）和 AI Fund（1.75 亿美元风险基金）。
Born 1976 in London to Hong Kong immigrant parents (father a hematologist, mother an arts administrator). Grew up in Singapore, attending the prestigious Raffles Institution. Earned a triple major in Computer Science, Statistics, and Economics from Carnegie Mellon (1997), an MS in EECS from MIT (1998), and a PhD in Computer Science from UC Berkeley (2002) under Michael I. Jordan. Joined Stanford as a professor (2002) where his CS229 Machine Learning course became the most popular class on campus (1000+ students). Co-founded Google Brain in 2011 with Jeff Dean -- their famous experiment trained a network on 16,000 CPUs that learned to recognize cats in YouTube videos. Co-founded Coursera (2012) with Daphne Koller, democratizing education for 8M+ students. Served as Chief Scientist at Baidu (2014-2017), leading 1,300 AI researchers. Founded DeepLearning.AI (2017), Landing AI (2018), and the AI Fund ($175M venture fund).
