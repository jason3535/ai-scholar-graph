剑桥大学基督学院学士（1997）。在阿尔伯塔大学获博士（2009），论文主题为强化学习和基于模拟的计算机围棋搜索，为后来的 AlphaGo 打下了基础。2010年起为 DeepMind 做顾问，2013年全职加入。领导强化学习研究组和 AlphaGo 项目，2016年击败世界冠军李世石（全球超过2亿人在线观看）。创造了 AlphaGo Zero（从零开始学习）和 AlphaZero（通过自我对弈精通国际象棋、将棋和围棋）。获 ACM 计算奖（2019），当选英国皇家学会院士（2021）。2026年1月离开 DeepMind，创办 Ineffable Intelligence。
Earned a BA from Christ's College, Cambridge (1997). Completed a PhD at the University of Alberta (2009), with a thesis on reinforcement learning and simulation-based search in computer Go -- laying the theoretical groundwork for what would become AlphaGo. Consulted for DeepMind from 2010, joined full-time in 2013. Led the reinforcement learning research group and the AlphaGo project that defeated world champion Lee Sedol in 2016 (watched live by 200M+ people). Created AlphaGo Zero (learning from scratch) and AlphaZero (mastering chess, shogi, and Go from self-play alone). Won the ACM Prize in Computing (2019) and was elected Fellow of the Royal Society (2021). Left DeepMind in January 2026 and founded Ineffable Intelligence.
