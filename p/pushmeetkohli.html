普什米特·科利 Pushmeet Kohli

Google DeepMind

Google DeepMind 研究副总裁 · AlphaFold/AlphaEvolve

deep-learningvision
IIT 孟买本科，牛津大学博士。先在微软剑桥研究院任首席研究员。2017年加入 DeepMind，升任研究副总裁。领导了 AlphaFold（蛋白质结构预测，革命性地改变了生物学）、AlphaEvolve（数学优化）、SynthID（AI 水印）和 AI 联合科学家等项目。入选 TIME 100 AI（2023）。DeepMind 最具影响力的研究领导者之一，架起 AI 与科学之间的桥梁。
IIT Bombay BTech, Oxford DPhil. Joined Microsoft Research Cambridge, became Principal Researcher. Moved to DeepMind in 2017, rose to VP of Research. Led teams behind AlphaFold (protein structure prediction, revolutionized biology), AlphaEvolve (mathematical optimization), SynthID (AI watermarking), and AI co-scientist. Named to TIME 100 AI (2023). One of DeepMind's most impactful research leaders, bridging AI and science.
