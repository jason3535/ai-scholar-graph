闫俊杰 Yan Junjie

MiniMax

MiniMax 创始人兼 CEO

nlpvision
约1989年生。高中时自学高等微积分。东南大学数学学士（2006），中科院自动化所博士（2015），清华大学博后。在商汤科技工作6年+，任研究院副院长。2019年因 OpenAI 的 Dota 2 AI 击败人类而着迷，从计算机视觉转向 NLP。2021年12月创办 MiniMax，开发海螺 AI（视频生成）和多模态模型套件。2026年1月在港交所 IPO，36岁成为亿万富翁，身家 32 亿美元。
Born ~1989. Taught himself advanced calculus in high school after finding the standard curriculum too slow. Earned a degree in Mathematics from Southeast University (2006), then a PhD from the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (2015). Conducted postdoctoral research at Tsinghua University. Spent 6+ years at SenseTime, rising to deputy head of its research institute. Became captivated by OpenAI after its bots defeated elite human teams in Dota 2 (2019), prompting a career pivot from computer vision to NLP. Founded MiniMax in December 2021. The company developed Hailuo AI (video generation) and a suite of multimodal models. MiniMax IPO'd on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange in January 2026, making Yan a billionaire at age 36 with a net worth of $3.2B.
