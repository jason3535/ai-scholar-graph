1986年生于捷克斯洛伐克布拉迪斯拉发（现斯洛伐克）。15岁移居多伦多。多伦多大学计算机科学与物理学学士（2009），UBC 硕士（2011），斯坦福博士（2015，导师 Fei-Fei Li，研究 NLP 与计算机视觉的交叉领域）。创建并教授了斯坦福传奇课程 CS 231n，从 150 人增长到 750 人。OpenAI 创始成员（2015-2017），后加入特斯拉担任 AI 和 Autopilot 视觉总监（2017-2022，直接向 Elon Musk 汇报）。短暂回归 OpenAI（2023-2024）。2024年7月创办 Eureka Labs，专注 AI 原生教育。也以 YouTube 频道和魔方教程闻名（900 万+观看）。入选 MIT 科技评论 35 岁以下创新者（2020）。
Born 1986 in Bratislava, Czechoslovakia (now Slovakia). Relocated to Toronto at age 15. Earned a BS in Computer Science and Physics from the University of Toronto (2009), MS from the University of British Columbia (2011), and PhD from Stanford (2015) under Fei-Fei Li, working at the intersection of NLP and computer vision. Created and taught Stanford's legendary CS 231n course, which grew from 150 to 750 students. Became a founding member of OpenAI (2015-2017), then joined Tesla as Director of AI and Autopilot Vision (2017-2022, reporting directly to Elon Musk). Returned briefly to OpenAI (2023-2024). Founded Eureka Labs (July 2024) to build AI-native education. Also known for his popular YouTube channel and Rubik's cube tutorials (9M+ views). Named MIT Tech Review Innovators Under 35 (2020).
