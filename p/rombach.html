Robin Rombach

Black Forest Labs

Stable Diffusion / Latent Diffusion 创造者

vision
德国 AI 研究者。海德堡大学物理学→LMU 慕尼黑计算机科学博士。合著了 Latent Diffusion 论文（2022），突破性地在压缩潜空间而非像素空间运行扩散——将计算成本降低 10-100 倍。这成为了 Stable Diffusion，最广泛使用的开源图像生成模型。曾任 Stability AI 研究总监。2024年与原始团队联合创办 Black Forest Labs，推出 FLUX 系列模型。
German AI researcher. Studied physics at Heidelberg University before transitioning to computer science. PhD at LMU Munich under Bjorn Ommer. Co-authored the Latent Diffusion paper (2022) which introduced the breakthrough idea of running diffusion in compressed latent space rather than pixel space -- reducing compute cost by 10-100x while maintaining quality. This became Stable Diffusion, the most widely used open-source image generation model. Joined Stability AI as research director. In 2024, left to co-found Black Forest Labs with the original latent diffusion team (Blattmann, Esser, Lorenz), creating the FLUX family of models that quickly became industry-leading image generators.
