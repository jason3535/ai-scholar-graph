nlp
1992年生于广东汕头。就读汕头金山中学，无编程背景被选入信息学奥赛训练队，获 NOIP 广东省一等奖，保送清华大学。初入热能工程系，大二转入计算机系，师从唐杰教授。2015年以全班第一毕业。赴卡内基梅隆大学读博，仅用4年完成（标准6年），2019年毕业，导师 Ruslan Salakhutdinov（后任 Apple AI 负责人）和 William Cohen（Google DeepMind）。读博期间在 Meta 和 Google Brain 实习。共同开发了 Transformer-XL 和 XLNet。2023年3月与清华同学周昕宇、吴育昕联合创办月之暗面。创造了 Kimi，中国最受欢迎的 AI 助手之一，以业界领先的长上下文能力著称（起步即 20万+ token）。同时兼任清华助理教授和上海期智研究院 PI。2026 年开源的 K2 / K2.5 模型带动营收大幅增长：1 月底起 20 天的销售额即超过 2025 全年；英伟达黄仁勋在 CES 2026 将 Kimi K2 列为对标的中国模型，杨植麟也成为唯一受邀在英伟达 GTC（2026.3.19）登台的中国独立大模型创始人、并在会上发布 K2.5。月之暗面已启动港股 IPO 筹备、估值剑指约 180 亿美元，账上现金约 100 亿元。
Born 1992 in Shantou, Guangdong. Attended Shantou Jinshan Middle School. With no prior programming background, was selected for the informatics Olympiad training program and won first prize in the Guangdong division of the National Olympiad in Informatics, earning guaranteed admission to Tsinghua University. Initially admitted to Thermal Energy Engineering, transferred to Computer Science in his sophomore year and studied under Professor Jie Tang. Graduated top of his class with a BSc (2015). Earned a PhD from Carnegie Mellon University in just 4 years (standard is 6), completed 2019, under Ruslan Salakhutdinov (later Apple's head of AI) and William Cohen (Google DeepMind). During his PhD, interned at Meta and Google Brain. Co-developed Transformer-XL and XLNet, extending Transformers to handle much longer sequences. Founded Moonshot AI in March 2023 with Tsinghua classmates Zhou Xinyu and Wu Yuxin. Created Kimi, one of China's most popular AI assistants, known for its industry-leading long-context capability (200K+ tokens from the start). Also an assistant professor at Tsinghua and PI at Shanghai Qizhi Research Institute. In 2026 Moonshot's open K2 / K2.5 models drove a revenue surge (sales in the first 20 days from late January topped all of 2025), and Nvidia's Jensen Huang cited Kimi K2 as a benchmark Chinese model at CES 2026. Yang was the only independent Chinese model-company founder to present at Nvidia's GTC (19 March 2026), where K2.5 debuted. Moonshot began preparing a Hong Kong IPO targeting roughly $18B, with ~RMB10B cash on hand.
