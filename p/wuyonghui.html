吴永辉 Wu Yonghui

ByteDance Seed

字节跳动 AI 研究负责人 · 前 Google Fellow

nlpdeep-learning
南京大学毕业，后在加州大学河滨分校获统计学硕士和计算机科学博士（2008）。2008年加入 Google，前7年做搜索排序，2014-2023年在 Google Brain 从事机器学习、基因组学和 NLP。2023年9月晋升 Google Fellow（相当于副总裁级别）。最知名成就是作为 Google 神经机器翻译系统（GNMT, 2016）第一作者，将 Google 翻译从短语级翻译升级为神经翻译。2025年2月加入字节跳动任 Seed 团队基础研究负责人，直接向 CEO 梁汝波汇报。还创造了 Seedance 2.0 视频生成模型。
Graduated from Nanjing University, then earned a Master's in Statistics and PhD in Computer Science from the University of California, Riverside (2008). Joined Google in 2008, spending his first 7 years as a software engineer on core search ranking. From 2014-2023 at Google Brain, working on machine learning, genomics, and NLP. Promoted to Google Fellow in September 2023 -- a senior-level position equivalent to VP. Best known as the first author of Google's Neural Machine Translation System (GNMT, 2016), which transformed Google Translate from phrase-based to neural. Joined ByteDance in February 2025 as head of foundational research for the Seed team (Doubao large model), reporting directly to CEO Liang Rubo. Also created Seedance 2.0, ByteDance's breakthrough video generation model.
