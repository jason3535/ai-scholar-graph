李彦宏 Robin Li

Baidu

百度创始人兼 CEO · ERNIE

nlp
1968年生于山西阳泉。北京大学信息管理学士，纽约州立大学布法罗分校计算机科学硕士。在美国创造了 RankDex（1996），一个使用超链接分析进行网页排名的早期搜索引擎——早于 Google 的 PageRank。2000年与徐勇联合创办百度。将百度大力转向 AI：自动驾驶平台 Apollo Go（累计超 2000 万次出行）、文心大模型（ERNIE）系列、文心一言（月活 2.02 亿）。入选 TIME 100 AI（2023）。
Born 1968 as Li Yanhong in Yangquan, Shanxi Province, China. Studied Information Management at Peking University, then earned an MS in Computer Science from the University at Buffalo, New York. While in the US, created RankDex (1996), an early search engine that used hyperlink analysis for page ranking -- predating Google's PageRank. Founded Baidu in 2000 with Eric Xu, growing it into China's dominant search engine. Has been CEO since 2004. Pivoted Baidu heavily toward AI: developed autonomous driving platform Apollo Go (20M+ rides by 2025), launched ERNIE (Wenxin) large language model series, and ERNIE Bot which reached 202M monthly active users by December 2025. Named to TIME 100 AI (2023). One of the earliest advocates for AI in China, betting on AI for over a decade before it became mainstream.
