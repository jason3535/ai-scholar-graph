Niki Parmar

Essential AI

Transformer 联合发明人

nlpvision
在 Google Brain 合著了《Attention Is All You Need》（2017）。在 Transformer 论文之后，率先将 Transformer 应用于图像生成（Image Transformer, 2018），帮助打通了 NLP 和计算机视觉架构的桥梁。与 Ashish Vaswani 联合创办 Essential AI，构建企业 AI 系统。
Co-author of 'Attention Is All You Need' (2017) at Google Brain. After the Transformer paper, pioneered applying Transformers to image generation with the Image Transformer (2018), helping bridge the gap between NLP and computer vision architectures. Co-founded Essential AI with Ashish Vaswani to build enterprise AI systems.
