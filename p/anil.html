Indian-born AI researcher who rose to become the technical lead of Google's most important AI project. Led the development of PaLM 2 and then the Gemini model family, Google's flagship multimodal AI system designed to compete with GPT-4. His work spans efficient training at scale, multilingual capabilities, and multimodal reasoning.
