新西兰裔 AI 研究者。在瑞士 IDSIA 跟随 Marcus Hutter 完成博士学位。在2007年的博士论文中，正式定义并创造了「通用人工智能」（AGI）这一术语，提供了衡量机器智能的数学框架。2010年与 Demis Hassabis 和 Mustafa Suleyman 联合创办 DeepMind。现任 Google DeepMind 首席 AGI 科学家，领导安全和 AGI 研究。
New Zealand-born AI researcher. Completed his PhD at IDSIA in Switzerland under Marcus Hutter. In his 2007 thesis, he formally defined and coined the term 'artificial general intelligence' (AGI), providing a mathematical framework for measuring machine intelligence. Co-founded DeepMind in 2010 with Demis Hassabis and Mustafa Suleyman. Now serves as Chief AGI Scientist at Google DeepMind, leading safety and AGI research.
