1963年生于慕尼黑。慕尼黑工业大学博士（1991）。瑞士 AI 实验室 IDSIA 联合主任。与 Sepp Hochreiter 联合发明了 LSTM（1997），在 Transformer 之前驱动了 Google 翻译、Siri、Alexa 和大多数序列建模。还较早研究了元学习、神经网络压缩和好奇心驱动学习。创办 NNAISENSE（2014）。2021年赴沙特 KAUST 领导 AI 计划。以在深度学习归功问题上的直言不讳著称。
Born 1963 in Munich, Germany. Earned his PhD from the Technical University of Munich (1991). Co-director of the Swiss AI Lab IDSIA in Lugano. Co-invented Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) networks with Sepp Hochreiter (1997) -- the architecture that powered Google Translate, Siri, Alexa, and most sequence modeling before Transformers. Also worked early on meta-learning, neural network compression, and formal theories of curiosity-driven learning. Founded NNAISENSE (2014) for industrial AI. Moved to KAUST in Saudi Arabia (2021) to lead the AI Initiative. Known for being vocal about credit attribution in deep learning history, frequently reminding the community of pre-2012 contributions.
