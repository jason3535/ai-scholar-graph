安德鲁·塔洛克 Andrew Tulloch

Meta Superintelligence Labs

Meta 超级智能实验室 · 前 Thinking Machines 联合创始人

deep-learning
澳大利亚机器学习研究者，曾长期任职于 Facebook AI Research 与 OpenAI，2025 年与 Mira Murati 共同创办 Thinking Machines Lab。2025 年 10 月离开，加入 Meta 超级智能实验室，向首席 AI 官 Alexandr Wang 汇报，据多方报道其薪酬包六年约 15 亿美元——被称为科技史上最贵的个人招募，是 2025-26 年 AI 人才战的标志性事件。专长于大规模机器学习系统与训练基础设施。
Australian machine-learning researcher who spent years at Facebook AI Research and OpenAI before co-founding Mira Murati's Thinking Machines Lab in 2025. In October 2025 he left to join Meta Superintelligence Labs, reporting to chief AI officer Alexandr Wang, in a deal widely reported at ~$1.5 billion over six years — described as the most expensive individual hire in tech history and a symbol of the 2025-26 AI talent war. A specialist in large-scale ML systems and training infrastructure.
