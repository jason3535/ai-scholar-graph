Diederik Kingma

Anthropic

VAE 和 Adam 优化器创造者

deep-learning
荷兰计算机科学家。阿姆斯特丹大学博士（2017，导师 Max Welling，最优等毕业）。创造了现代 AI 两大基础构件：VAE（2013，连接概率建模与深度学习）和 Adam 优化器（2014，与 Jimmy Ba 合著，95000+引用）。OpenAI 创始团队成员（2015）。Google Brain 研究员。2024年10月加入 Anthropic。连续获得 ICLR 时间检验奖（2024 VAE、2025 Adam）。
Dutch computer scientist. PhD cum laude from the University of Amsterdam (2017) under Max Welling. Created two of the most fundamental building blocks of modern AI: the Variational Autoencoder (VAE, 2013) -- bridging probabilistic modeling and deep learning, enabling generative models to learn latent structure -- and the Adam optimizer (2014, with Jimmy Ba) -- the most widely used optimization algorithm in deep learning with 95,000+ citations. Part of the founding team of OpenAI (2015). Worked at Google Brain on large-scale optimization and generative models. Joined Anthropic in October 2024. Won the ICLR Test of Time Award in 2024 (VAE) and 2025 (Adam) -- two consecutive years.
