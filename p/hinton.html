Geoffrey Hinton

University of Toronto

深度学习之父 · 2018 图灵奖 · 2024 诺贝尔物理学奖

deep-learning
1947年生于伦敦温布尔登。就读于布里斯托尔的 Clifton College，后在剑桥大学国王学院获实验心理学学士（1970）。曾中断学业一年去当木匠学徒。在爱丁堡大学获得 AI 方向博士（1978），导师 Christopher Longuet-Higgins。先后在苏塞克斯大学做博后、在卡内基梅隆大学参与 PDP 小组（1980年代）。1987年加入多伦多大学至今。发表了具有里程碑意义的反向传播论文（1986, Nature，与 Rumelhart & Williams 合著），提出玻尔兹曼机（1985）、深度信念网络（2006），并与 Krizhevsky 和 Sutskever 合著 AlexNet（2012），以历史性优势赢得 ImageNet 竞赛，引爆深度学习革命。2017年联合创办 Vector Institute。2013年加入 Google Brain，2023年5月辞职以自由发声警示 AI 风险。与 LeCun 和 Bengio 共享2018图灵奖。2024年与 Hopfield 共获诺贝尔物理学奖。
Born 1947 in London. Studied at Clifton College, then earned a BA in Experimental Psychology at King's College Cambridge (1970). After a year apprenticing as a carpenter, pursued a PhD in AI at the University of Edinburgh (1978) under Christopher Longuet-Higgins. Postdoc at Sussex, then joined Carnegie Mellon in the 1980s as part of the PDP (Parallel Distributed Processing) group. Moved to the University of Toronto in 1987 where he has remained ever since. Published the landmark backpropagation paper (1986, Nature) with Rumelhart & Williams. Introduced Boltzmann machines (1985), deep belief nets (2006), and co-authored AlexNet (2012) with Krizhevsky and Sutskever, which won ImageNet by a historic margin and ignited the deep learning revolution. Co-founded the Vector Institute (2017). Joined Google Brain in 2013, resigned in May 2023 to speak freely about AI risks. Shared the Turing Award (2018) with LeCun and Bengio. Awarded the Nobel Prize in Physics (2024) alongside John Hopfield.
