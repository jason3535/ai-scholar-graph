计算机图形与视觉研究者，在斯坦福获学士（2015），在 UC Berkeley 获博士（2020），导师为 Ren Ng。他是 2020 年 NeRF（神经辐射场）论文的第一作者，推动了现代神经渲染和基于图像的 3D 重建。2021 至 2023 年在 Google 担任研究科学家，与 Jonathan Barron 合作产出 Mip-NeRF、Block-NeRF、DreamFusion、Zip-NeRF。2024 年联合创办由李飞飞发起的空间智能公司 World Labs。
Computer graphics and vision researcher who earned a BS from Stanford (2015) and a PhD from UC Berkeley (2020) advised by Ren Ng. He was the lead author of the 2020 NeRF (Neural Radiance Fields) paper,which catalyzed the modern neural-rendering field and 3D reconstruction from images. From 2021 to 2023 he was a research scientist at Google,collaborating with Jonathan Barron on Mip-NeRF,Block-NeRF,DreamFusion and Zip-NeRF. In 2024 he co-founded World Labs,the spatial-intelligence company launched by Fei-Fei Li.
