1993年4月生。毕业于 Olin College of Engineering（马萨诸塞州 Needham 的一所小型工程学院）。约2016年加入 OpenAI，成为 AI 史上个人影响力最大的贡献者之一。GPT-1（2018）第一作者，证明了生成式预训练能产生强大的语言理解能力。随后推出 GPT-2（2019，因担心滥用风险曾暂缓发布）。又主导了 DALL-E（文生图）、CLIP（视觉-语言连接）和 Whisper（语音识别）。他的工作本质上创造了整个 GPT 时代的基础。2024年12月离开 OpenAI，2025年成为 Thinking Machines Lab 顾问。
Born April 1993. Graduated from Olin College of Engineering, a small engineering school in Needham, Massachusetts. Joined OpenAI around 2016 and became one of the most impactful individual contributors in the history of AI. Lead author of GPT-1 (2018), which demonstrated that generative pre-training could produce strong language understanding. Followed with GPT-2 (2019), which was initially withheld due to concerns about misuse. Went on to lead DALL-E (text-to-image generation), CLIP (connecting vision and language), and Whisper (speech recognition). His work essentially created the foundation for the entire GPT era. Left OpenAI in December 2024 and became an advisor at Thinking Machines Lab (2025).
