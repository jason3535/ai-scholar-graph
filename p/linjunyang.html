林骏扬 Lin Junyang

Independent

前 Qwen 技术负责人 · 阿里最年轻 P10

nlp
1993年生。北京大学计算机和语言学专业，语言学硕士。2019年加入阿里达摩院任高级算法工程师。主导了阿里 M6（超大规模 MoE 模型）、OFA（ICML 2022）和 Chinese-CLIP。最核心成就是将 Qwen 从一个不知名的副项目打造成全球最有影响力的开源大模型系列。2025年成为阿里最年轻的 P10 级技术负责人。2026年3月因不认同将 Qwen 团队垂直整合结构拆散的组织重组方案而辞职。
Born 1993. Studied computer science and linguistics at Peking University, earning a Master's degree in linguistics. Joined Alibaba's DAMO Academy in 2019 as a Senior Algorithm Engineer working on NLP and multimodal research. Played central roles in Alibaba's M6 (extreme-scale MoE model), OFA (ICML 2022), and Chinese-CLIP. His defining achievement was turning Qwen from an unknown side project into the world's most influential open-source LLM series. Became the youngest P10-level tech lead at Alibaba (2025) -- an extraordinarily senior technical position. Resigned in March 2026 over disagreements with organizational restructuring that would fragment the vertically integrated Qwen team structure he had built.
