计算机视觉研究者，在斯坦福获博士学位，导师为李飞飞，期间共同讲授了 CS231n 深度学习课程。主要工作包括用于实时风格迁移的感知损失与 CLEVR 视觉推理数据集。后任密歇根大学助理教授，同时担任 Facebook AI Research 研究科学家。2024 年与昔日导师李飞飞等人共同创办 World Labs，构建面向空间智能的「大世界模型」；该公司融资超 10 亿美元，并发布 Marble 世界生成模型。
Computer vision researcher who earned his PhD at Stanford advised by Fei-Fei Li,where he co-taught the CS231n deep learning course. His work includes perceptual losses for real-time style transfer and the CLEVR visual reasoning dataset. He became an assistant professor at the University of Michigan while also working as a research scientist at Facebook AI Research. In 2024 he co-founded World Labs with his former advisor Fei-Fei Li to build 'large world models' for spatial intelligence; the startup raised over $1B and launched its Marble world-generation model.
